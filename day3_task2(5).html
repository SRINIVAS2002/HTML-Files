About Trivikram Srinivas

I also used to watch repeatedly some movies because of dialogues consisting very meaningful in a rhyming manner. 

Those kind of dialogues written by Trivikram Srinivas, also famously known as Guruji. 

He is said to be a magician for the meaningful dialogues and generating fun, drama, emotion feels like getting connected to the movie automatically.

Trivikram Srinivas, oftenly made a mark for his unique writing style in TFI(Telugu Film Industry). 

Trivikram’s films are known for their clever humor, which is woven seamlessly into the narrative. His dialogues often contain witty wordplay and situational comedy that appeal to a wide audience.

Many of his films revolve around family dynamics, often focusing on broken families. This recurring theme resonates with audiences, as it explores the complexities of familial relationships.

Trivikram’s scripts often include multiple subplots that develop alongside the main story. This adds depth to his films and keeps the audience engaged with various character arcs and mini-stories.

His dialogues are not only humorous but also thought-provoking and emotionally impactful.

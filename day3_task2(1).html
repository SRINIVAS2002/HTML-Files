About Mahesh Babu

From my childhood days, I used to watch Super Star Mahesh Babu movies many more times which will not get bored.
 
Even though I am a fan of Stylish Star Allu Arjun, I liked movies of Mahesh Babu a lot.
 
He absolutely look handsome and his charismatic smile style made fans go crazy.

He has adopted two villages, one is Burripalem in Andhra Pradesh and another one is Siddhapuram in Telangana, and involved in their development, focusing on improving infrastructure, healthcare, and education.

He has also been associated with the Heal-a-Child Foundation, which helps children from underprivileged backgrounds receive medical treatment.

There have been numerous accounts from fans and colleagues about his humble and down-to-earth nature. 

For instance, during the COVID-19 pandemic, he contributed significantly to relief efforts and supported daily wage workers in the TFI(Telugu Film Industry).

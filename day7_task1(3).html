Chapter 1: Actual name and identity

Suhas Pagolu is an Indian actor who works in Telugu films.
He initially appeared in short films and supporting roles, and made his debut in a lead role with Colour Photo (2020).

Chapter 2: Family and DOB

Suhas was born in 19 August 1990 and brought up in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh.
Suhas father working as a Police Officer in Vijayawada and his mother as a home maker.
He has an elder brother name Pramod Pagolu.
He got married with Lalitha on 26 August 2017.

Chapter 3: Career beginning stage

Suhas relocated to Hyderabad in order to pursue a career in movies after earning his degree from KBN College.
He first appeared in a Telugu YouTube channel called Chai Bisket and went on to appear in a number of short films like The Athidhi.
Suhas made his acting debut in the 2018 film Padi Padi Leche Manasu.

Chapter 4: Movies which made successful

He has since appeared in a number of supporting parts, including Agent Sai Srinivasa Athreya (2019).
Suhas makes an amazing appearance as Detective Bobby, according to a critic for The Times of India who gave the movie a positive review.
He made an appearance in Majili (2019), which is why “Majili Suhas” is occasionally used to refer to him.
Alongside Satyadev Kancharana, he repeated Soubin Shahir's role from the Telugu version of Maheshinte Prathikaaram, Uma Maheswara Ugra Roopasya. Then Suhas worked on Color Photo with his closest buddy Sandeep Raj. With this movie, he made his acting debut in a leading role.
